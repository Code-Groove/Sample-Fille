WORLD WAR TWO

THE BEGINNINGS

World War Two actually began in 1918 when Germany lost the FIRST WORLD WAR. Everyone blamed Germany for their defeat in the war. Germany had to pay huge amounts of taxes to Britain, France, Russia and all the other allies. Most of their industrially productive parts were snatched by the allies. Watching all of this was Adolf Hitler. He had been a brave soldier in the German Wehrmacht. As a soldier, he was awarded the Iron Cross for his bravery. Then Hitler became one of the many soldiers who were left with no job after the war as the German armed forces were disbanded. He vowed revenge. This was one of the main reasons why he had become a dictator later in his life.

The allies were harsh on Germany. As a result, there was an Economic Depression whe people were begging for basic amenities like food, water, clothing and money. There were millions out on the street begigngg for employment. In this situation, the German Parliament, or the Weimar Republic recklessly printed currency. The value of the German Mark fell.

From such a crisis emerged the NATIONAL GERMAN SOCIALIST PARTY or the NAZI PARTY. In 1923, Hitler became the leader of the party. In a neighbouring country to Germany, Bavaria, Hitler launched a military coup and overthrew the Bavarian Government. So, he was jailed for 5 years. In jail, he wrote his book, Mein Kampf or My Struggle in jail. When he was released in 1929, he was welcomed with great cheer in Germany. Four years later, he was offered Chancellorship of Germany. Hitler agreed. The first thing he did was suspend all civil rights and banning all organizations except the Nazi Party. He established the Gestapo, secret police who identified and eliminated people who spoke against the Nazis.

After the First World War, the Allies had disbanded the German Armed Forces. Hitler rebuilt them tto their full force. They were called The Wehrmacht(land army), the Kriegsmarine(the Navy) and The Luftwaffe(the air force)

Hitler worked on the principle "survivial of the fittest". He felt only those who were born in the Pure-Nordic Aryan race had thw right to live. He racially discriminated Jews, Poles, Blacks, cripples and his own people born with physical disabilities.

He started the HOLOCAUST where innocent civilians were rounded up and sent to gas chambers to die. It was a brutal act where alomst 8-13 million people died in the span of 12 years. (1933-1945)
